About Me

I used to work with language. Now I work with code.

My journey into software development began when I chose to leave a career I truly valued: translation. Not because the field was outdated, but because I felt I had stopped growing. I wanted something different — something that would challenge me and keep doing so.

I found that in coding. Today, I learn something new almost every day. And with the ever-evolving nature of tech, I don’t see that changing anytime soon.

Through my education, I built a strong foundation in Java and Spring Boot, along with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Vue. And during my traineeship, I worked hands-on with microservices and modern DevOps tools.

Now, I’m looking to join a team where I can keep growing as a developer and as a person. Ideally, I’d like to work on-site, collaborating closely with others to build great products — and share a few laughs along the way.
